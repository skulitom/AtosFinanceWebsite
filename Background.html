ATOS

ATOS was first formed back in 1997 when two french IT companies merged together, it was later rebranded into ATOS Origin after more companies merged in the year 2000. The current headquarters of ATOS are located in Bezons France and Munich Germany. We are specifically working with the branch that is located in London Euston however our client spends most of the time working for ATOS in Manchester.

Currently ATOS provides a number of services which includes but is not limited to:

  • consulting & systems integration
  • managed services
  • cloud with its structure Canopy
  • big data and analytics
  • cybersecurity
  • payment and e-transactional services from its subsidiary Worldline
  • unified communication and colloration services and products from the Unify acquisition

NS&I

NS&I is an investment and savings bank which is state owned by the UK government and was first formed back in 1861. Historically the aim of NS&I was to attract funds from individual savers in UK for the purpose of funding the government public sector. Since NS&I is a government owned organisation it offers quite a few services that are tax free, this differentiates NS&I from quite a few other savings companies that are located in the UK.
